Steve Jobs: The Visionary

Steve Jobs🌹

1955 - 2011

"Stay Hungry. Stay Foolish."

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur, industrial designer, and media proprietor. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc. Through Apple, he revolutionized the computer, music, and phone industries with products like the Macintosh, iPod, and iPhone.

His focus on1 minimalistic design and user experience changed how the world interacts with technology.
